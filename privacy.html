Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

HairBack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the HairBack app ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive the display name and email (if you choose to share it).
  • Photos: You upload photos of your scalp/hair for AI analysis. These are processed to generate density insights.
  • Hair goals and preferences: Information you select for your profile, such as treatment goals and weak points.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data: General usage of the App to improve our services.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version for compatibility and support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the App (routines, recommendations, progress tracking)
  • Run AI-based hair analysis on your photos
  • Process subscriptions and support
  • Improve the App and fix issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. AI and Photo Processing

Photos you submit for hair analysis are sent to secure cloud services for AI processing. Processing is used solely to analyze hair density and generate your personalized insights. We do not use your photos for advertising, training unrelated models, or sharing with third parties for marketing.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Routine data, progress history, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Processed analysis results may be stored to support your progress history. Photos sent for analysis are processed and not retained longer than necessary to deliver results, unless you have opted into features that require retention. We retain your data only as long as needed to provide the App and meet legal requirements.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with service providers (e.g., cloud AI, analytics, payment processing) who act on our behalf under strict agreements, when required by law, or in connection with a merger or acquisition (with notice).

6. Apple Sign-In

If you sign in with Apple, Apple's Privacy Policy also applies to your sign-in. We receive only the information you authorize Apple to share with us.

7. Subscriptions and Payments

Subscription purchases are handled by Apple. We receive transaction and subscription status information to enable access to premium features. Payment details are not stored or processed by us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your data, and to data portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at partnerships@hairback.app. You can also delete your account and data from within the App or by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

10. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure communication. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place where required by law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the App or by email where possible. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights: partnerships@hairback.app
